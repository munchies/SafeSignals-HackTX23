~ Instructions ~

1. Choose a word (less than 16 letters long) in English that you want to display.

2. Use the Morse Code reference sheet to learn how to convert the English letters to Morse Code.

3. Turn the Rotary so that the yellow LED is on. This means that the system is ready to take inputs.

4. Use the button and the touch sensor to type out dots and dashes: a push on the button represents a dot, and a tap on the touch sensor represents a dash.

5. Enter one letter in Morse Code (up to 4 dashes and/or dots).

6. After you have finished enter a letter, turn the rotary to the left, until the green LED turns on.

7. If the buzzer goes off, you have entered an invalid letter. It will reset your attempt, and you can try again.

8. If you have more letters to enter, turn the rotary to the right until the yellow LED turns on. Then, Repeat steps 6-8.

9. If you have finished entering your word, turn the rotary all the way to the right, until the red LED turns on.

10. Your word will display on the screen, and then it will reset so that you can practice some more!
